What we offer

Everything you need,
nothing you don’t.

We keep it close to vanilla with just enough quality-of-life improvements to make your survival experience smoother and more rewarding.

Auction House

A fully player-driven economy. List items, set buy orders, and browse hundreds of listings — all in-game.

Crates

Earn crate keys through voting and gameplay. Spin for rare items, custom tools, and exclusive rewards.

Player Homes

Set homes at your base, your farm, and your mine. Teleport back any time with a single command.

AFK Zone

Step into the dedicated AFK zone to keep your farms running without a worry. Your progress never stops.

Community Roles

Earn your rank by playing and contributing. Rise through the community ranks with real, visible recognition.

Live Scoreboard

A real-time in-game scoreboard showing your balance, world, and ping. Always know where you stand.
