Server Rules

By playing on MostlyVanilla or joining our Discord, you agree to follow these rules. Read them — they exist to protect everyone.

General Rules
1
Use Common Sense
If something gives an unfair advantage, ruins gameplay for others, or is clearly unintended — don't do it. When in doubt, ask staff.
2
Respect Everyone
Excessive toxicity, harassment, targeted griefing, or intentionally trying to ruin the server experience for any player may result in punishment.
3
Staff Decisions Are Final
During active situations and events, staff decisions are final. If you believe a decision was unfair, open a Discord ticket — don't argue in chat.
Cheating & Exploits
NO
No Hacking or Unfair Advantages
Hacked clients, cheats, exploits, or any external programs that provide an unfair advantage are strictly prohibited.
Prohibited modifications include:
  • X-Ray or resource packs that reveal ores/structures
  • ESP, tracers, radar, or player/entity location mods
  • Kill aura, reach, aim assist, trigger bot, auto crystal, velocity, or movement hacks
  • Fly hacks, speed hacks, timer manipulation, or no-clip
  • Auto-clickers or macros that provide combat/mining advantages
  • Baritone or automated pathfinding/botting software
  • Freecam or spectator-style camera exploits

Unsure if your mod is allowed? Ask staff before using it.

NO
No Duplication
Any form of item, block, or entity duplication is prohibited — intentional or otherwise.
  • Known duplication glitches
  • Plugin or server exploit abuse
  • Intentional lag machines used for duplication
  • Attempting to crash or overload the server

If you accidentally discover a dupe, report it immediately — don't abuse it.

NO
No Bug Exploiting
Abusing unintended game mechanics, glitches, or server bugs for personal gain is not allowed.
  • Glitching through blocks or protected areas
  • Bypassing PvP or combat restrictions
  • Using chunk bugs or rendering exploits to locate bases
  • Abuse of server lag or crashes
  • Any exploit staff determine to be unfair or game-breaking
Combat Rules
NO
No Elytra in Combat
Using an elytra during active PvP combat is not permitted. Keep fights fair and on the ground.
NO
No Crystal PvP
End crystals may not be used as weapons against players in any form.
  • Crystal aura or automated crystal combat
  • Manual crystal combat against players
  • Crystal traps intended to kill players
  • Using crystals during raids or fights

Crystals may only be used for legitimate purposes such as respawning the Ender Dragon, unless otherwise stated by staff.

NO
No Combat Logging
Logging out, disconnecting, or otherwise leaving the game to avoid death or combat is prohibited.
  • Disconnecting during or immediately before a fight
  • Leaving while being chased or trapped
  • Using alternate accounts to avoid combat consequences

If you are in combat, finish the fight before leaving the server.

Punishments
Warning
First offense for minor rule violations
Temporary Ban
Repeated or moderate violations
Permanent Ban
Severe offenses or repeat violations

Note: Severe offenses such as hacking, duping, or major exploit abuse may result in immediate permanent punishment without prior warning. Staff have the final say.

1
Respect Others
Treat all members with respect. Hate speech, racism, sexual harassment, personal attacks, threats, impersonation, targeted abuse, or trolling of any kind will not be tolerated.
2
Protect Privacy
Do not share personal information — yours or anyone else's — whether publicly or privately. This includes names, addresses, phone numbers, photos, or any identifying data.
3
Sensitive Topics
Avoid discussions involving controversial, dangerous, or illegal topics. This includes politics, religion, and anything that may incite conflict or discomfort within the community.
4
No Spam or Disruptive Behavior
Spamming in any form is prohibited:
  • Rapid messaging or flooding chat
  • Excessive use of caps or emojis
  • Unsolicited mentions — especially staff
  • NSFW content or bypassing filters
5
No Advertising
Advertising other Discord servers, payment links, services, or social media is not allowed without explicit permission from staff.
6
Use Channels Properly
Use each channel for its intended purpose. Keep all communication in English only, unless otherwise specified.
7
Appropriate Profiles
Your username, profile picture, and status must be appropriate for all audiences. Inappropriate or offensive content will result in action.
8
Punishment Evasion
Do not use alternate accounts or any other means to evade bans or mutes. Doing so will result in further and more severe action.
9
Interacting with Staff
If you believe a staff member acted unfairly, open a ticket instead of arguing in chat. Do not ping multiple staff members unnecessarily.
By being in our Discord, you also agree to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Questions or Appeals?

If you were banned and believe it was a mistake, or have a question about a specific rule, open a ticket in Discord and a staff member will respond promptly.
